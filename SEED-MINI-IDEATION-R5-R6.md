## The Breaker: Cage's 4'33"
Scores 0/10 on all 4 orders but is genius. Important edge case:
- Empty performances need a special diagnostic: "SILENCE" category
- The diagnostic should recognize intentional silence vs no input
- 4'33" is actually the ultimate constraint: the ONLY constraint is time
