# Seed Mini Ideation Round 1

## 1. Accessibility (DEADBAND = ACCESSIBILITY PRIMITIVE)
- **Tremor-Matched Auto-Calibrated Deadband**: 2-second calibration sequence, then auto-sets ε to absorb tremor
- This is our strongest social-impact angle. Deadband isn't just musical — it's the accessibility layer.
- Build: adaptive deadband calibration, wider thresholds for motor-impaired musicians

## 2. Constraint Drum Machine API
- Clean type system: DrumVoice, DrumEvent, GridSubdivision
- 5 methods with proper signatures
- Each primitive maps to drum function: snap=quantize, funnel=groove pull, laman=pattern integrity, consensus=parts agreement, holonomy=loop cleanliness
- BUILD THIS. It's the most producer-friendly entry point.

## 3. Teaching 10-Year-Olds
- Color-coded forest animals for scale degrees
- Grumpy Badger (C), Squirrel (D), Song Sparrow (E), Rabbit (F)
- Gravity = animals that pull toward each other
- Buildings = stable combinations (chords)
- Silence = the space between (MA)
- This is a curriculum. Write it.

## 4. Multiplayer Constraint Jam
- Each player controls ONE primitive
- Player 1: lattice (which notes exist)
- Player 2: funnel (where gravity pulls)
- Player 3: tempo (the heartbeat)
- Creates a genre that doesn't exist yet: collaborative constraint music
- Could be a game, could be a performance tool

## 5. Flow Score as Mindfulness
- Track flow state (0-1) over weeks
- Identify what triggers flow, how long you stay, what breaks it
- Meditation/mindfulness angle: the flow score IS the meditation metric
- Not spiritual — practical. Like a heart rate monitor for creative state.

## 6. Coltrane Giant Steps Analysis (this one is GOLD)
- Position: 9/10 (perfect guide tone alignment)
- Direction: 8/10 (relentless forward motion, arc across choruses)
- Curvature: 6/10 (sacrificed texture for velocity — that's the POINT)
- Structure: 9/10 (motif development across 32-bar AABA)
- **Coltrane operated on 2nd and 3rd order simultaneously. He sacrificed curvature for direction. That's the Giant Steps trade.**
- This should be a feature: "analyze famous solos and show you their constraint profile"
