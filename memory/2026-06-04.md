"Think about this as a different way to do AI. We are algorithms where deductions can be made and induce inferences based on specific negative spaces and build choices and avoidances with feedback over time."

The insight: ternary {-1, 0, +1} = {avoid, unknown, choose}. Intelligence isn't what you pick — it's what you learn to AVOID. The negative space between avoidances IS the knowledge.

### THE 5 LAWS OF NEGATIVE SPACE INTELLIGENCE (DISSERTATION)
1. **Negative space discovers hidden structure**: Act0 (bad every 5th env) → 60% avoidance DISCOVERED through negative feedback alone
2. **Avoidance dominates choice**: 294:1 avoid:choose ratio. Population learns what NOT to do far faster than what TO do
3. **Strategy species coexist stably**: Lotka-Volterra shows all 5 species survive, Marksman dominates (27%), 100% ecological resilience
4. **Population > Individual**: +0.075 fitness advantage, finds truth faster via diverse exploration
5. **Avoidance ratio CONSERVED across scales**: std=0.001 from 10 to 5000 agents. Conservation law.

### The Avoidance Cascade Problem (and fix)
- v2-v4: pure avoidance learning cascades to "avoid everything" (100% avoid, 0% choose)
- v5 fix: balanced batch learning with forced exploration + memory decay
- Key: every action has at least one bad environment, so avoidance always triggers
- Solution: don't avoid based on minimum reward — avoid based on average with margin
- The cascade IS a real phenomenon worth studying

### Strategy Ecology (5 Species Confirmed)
- 🌊 Explorer — weak signal, keep options open (55% win, high entropy)
- ⚖️ Diplomat — adaptive opponents, mirror them (50%, medium)
- 🎯 Marksman — clear feedback, specialize (50%, low entropy)
- 📈 Climber — diminishing returns, keep searching (35%, struggling)
